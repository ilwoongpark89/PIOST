About PIOST

Replacing Fossil-Fuel Boilers with Clean, Renewable-Energy Based Heating

PIOST Inc. is revolutionizing thermal energy storage through innovative heating and hot-water supply systems. Our technology enables long-duration heat supply by storing renewable electricity as heat energy, making sustainable heating a reality.

We are demonstrating our TES systems in Mongolia and preparing for European market entry, focusing on decarbonizing heating to complete the global energy transition.
